Securing the Nation Through Innovation

1958

Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO)

Established to enhance India's defense capabilities through indigenous research and development, DRDO has become one of the world's leading defense research organizations.

1980s

Integrated Guided Missile Development Programme

Under Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam's leadership, India developed indigenous missile systems including Prithvi, Agni, Akash, Nag, and Trishul, establishing missile technology capabilities.

1989

Prithvi Missile Success

India's first indigenously developed ballistic missile demonstrated the country's capability in strategic weapons systems and tactical battlefield support.

1999

Kargil War Technology Applications

The Kargil conflict showcased India's defense technologies including precision artillery, surveillance systems, and communication networks in high-altitude warfare.

2003

BrahMos Supersonic Cruise Missile

Joint development with Russia produced the world's fastest supersonic cruise missile, demonstrating India's advanced missile technology and international cooperation capabilities.

2008

Agni-III Long Range Missile

Successful testing of Agni-III intermediate-range ballistic missile extended India's strategic reach and established credible deterrence capabilities.

2012

Agni-V Intercontinental Ballistic Missile

India joined the exclusive club of nations with ICBM capability, demonstrating advanced rocket technology and strategic defense systems.

2019

Anti-Satellite (ASAT) Weapon Test

Mission Shakti demonstrated India's capability to target satellites in space, establishing the country as the fourth nation with proven ASAT technology.

2020

Indigenous Aircraft Carrier INS Vikrant

India commissioned its first indigenously built aircraft carrier, showcasing advanced naval engineering and shipbuilding capabilities.

Light Combat Aircraft (LCA) Tejas

India's indigenous fighter aircraft achieved operational status, demonstrating advanced aerospace engineering and reducing dependence on foreign defense imports.

2022

Make in India Defense Initiative

India's push for defense manufacturing self-reliance has led to increased indigenous production, technology transfer, and reduced defense imports.

Present

Advanced Defense Technologies

India is developing next-generation technologies including hypersonic missiles, directed energy weapons, artificial intelligence in defense, and unmanned combat systems.

Future

Defense Vision 2047

India aims to become a global defense manufacturing hub, achieve complete self-reliance in critical defense technologies, and export advanced defense systems worldwide.

Strategic Achievements

India's defense technology journey from dependence to self-reliance demonstrates the nation's commitment to sovereignty and technological excellence. Today, India stands among the world's leading defense technology nations.
